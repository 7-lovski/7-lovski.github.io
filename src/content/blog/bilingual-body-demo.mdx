</Fragment>
<Fragment slot="en">

1. Frontmatter fields such as title and description remain localized with `zh / en` values.
2. The body can now be written in paired segments instead of one full Chinese block followed by one full English block.

</Fragment>
</LocalizedProse>

<Fragment slot="zh">

## 这样做的好处

<Fragment slot="en">

## Why this is useful

</Fragment>
</LocalizedProse>

<LocalizedProse>
<Fragment slot="zh">

- 内容文件还是只有一份。
- 双语路由下会按中文一段、英文一段的节奏阅读。
- `/zh/...` 和 `/en/...` 仍然只显示对应语言的正文。

</Fragment>
<Fragment slot="en">

- There is still only one content file to maintain.
- On the bilingual route, the reading rhythm becomes one Chinese segment followed by its matching English segment.
- `/zh/...` and `/en/...` still render only the matching language body.

</Fragment>
</LocalizedProse>

<Fragment slot="en">

## Writing Pattern

</Fragment>

- 一段中文

- 下一段再继续成对写

</Fragment>
<Fragment slot="en">

From here on, you can write content with this rhythm:

- one Chinese segment
- its matching English segment right after it
- then the next pair

</Fragment>
</LocalizedProse>

<LocalizedProse>
<Fragment slot="zh">

这样更适合项目案例、过程说明和复盘内容，因为你不用在整篇写完中文之后，再回头补整篇英文。

</Fragment>
<Fragment slot="en">

This works especially well for project case studies, process notes, and retrospectives, because you no longer need to finish the entire Chinese version before coming back for the full English version.

</Fragment>
